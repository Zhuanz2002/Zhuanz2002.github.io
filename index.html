01 / ONSIGHT

OnSight

On-device vision-language support for contextual and privacy-preserving navigation in blind mobility.

OnSight combines first-person camera input, coarse GPS and route context, an on-device FastVLM, temporal filtering, and concise spoken feedback. It augments established mobility tools with timely cues about landmarks, openings, surface changes, entrances, and scene structure.

01 · System

A complete sensing-to-speech loop

Rear-camera frames and CoreLocation data feed local inference and route reasoning. Lookahead, deviation smoothing, action voting, duplicate suppression, and speech scheduling keep guidance selective and timely.

02 · Evidence

Evaluated in real-world mobility tasks

The paper reports a counterbalanced study with 13 blind adults using an iPhone 16 Pro across four outdoor route segments and indoor exploration tasks, comparing on-device and cloud-backed conditions.

03 · Next

Navigate and Explore, designed separately

The next cycle separates terse, event-driven route guidance from deliberate target-focused exploration, while improving route state, uncertainty, accessibility, privacy controls, replay testing, and model specialization.
